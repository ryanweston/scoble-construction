ABOUT US

M. Scoble Construction Ltd started business in 1997 working as a Main Contractor for National Developers on sites in Devon, Cornwall & Somerset.

M. Scoble Construction Ltd has expanded over the years providing a quality service in Groundworks, Civil Engineering & building to National Contractors such as CJ Fry, Redrow Homes, Mansell Construction Services, Leadbitter, Galliford Try, Taylor Wimpey, Barratt Homes, Wainhomes & Pearce Construction.

We have successfully completed sites as Main and Principle Contractors depending on the requirements and have been involved with service contracts to major retail providers.

M. Scoble Construction Ltd prides itself in a personal service delivering projects within deadlines, assuring the best service to our customers at all times.

We have a team of dedicated, hardworking and loyal staff, trained in CITB, SMSTS, CSCS, CPCS and South West Water Approved.

Our projects range from Greenfield to Brownfield Sites, continuing through to the end of the project and finalising the project with external works.

M. Scoble Construction Ltd Company Accreditations include CHAS & SMAS.
